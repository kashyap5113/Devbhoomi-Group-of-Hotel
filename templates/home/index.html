About Dwarka

The sacred city of Lord Krishna

The Ancient Kingdom of Krishna

Dwarka is one of the four sacred Hindu pilgrimage sites known as Char Dham. Located on the western tip of Gujarat, this ancient city holds immense religious significance as the legendary kingdom of Lord Krishna.

The magnificent Dwarkadhish Temple, dedicated to Lord Krishna, stands as the centerpiece of this holy city. With its intricate architecture and spiritual aura, it attracts millions of devotees and tourists every year.

Beyond its religious importance, Dwarka offers serene beaches, the beautiful Gomti Ghat, and the nearby island of Beyt Dwarka, making it a perfect blend of spirituality and natural beauty.
